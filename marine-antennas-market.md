<p><p>The Marine Antennas market comprises two primary types: fixed-mount and handheld antennas. Fixed-mount antennas are permanently installed on ships and boats, providing robust communication capabilities for long-range naval operations. These are typically used for applications requiring stable connectivity, such as navigation and satellite communication. In contrast, handheld antennas offer portability and convenience for individual users, ideal for smaller vessels or for use during shore excursions. Both types cater to diverse maritime communication needs, enhancing safety and operational efficiency.</p></p>
