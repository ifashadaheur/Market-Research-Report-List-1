<p><ul><li>Primary Injection Test Equipment</li><li>Secondary Injection Test Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Injection Test Equipment market is primarily divided into two categories: Primary Injection Test Equipment and Secondary Injection Test Equipment. Primary Injection Test Equipment is designed for testing the performance of protective relays by applying current directly to the relay's input. In contrast, Secondary Injection Test Equipment tests relays by simulating their input signals without applying high current directly, allowing for safer and more controlled testing environments. Both types are essential for ensuring the reliability and accuracy of electrical protection systems.</p></p>
