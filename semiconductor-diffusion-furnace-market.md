<p><p>The semiconductor diffusion furnace market comprises mainly two types: vertical and horizontal furnaces. Vertical furnaces facilitate uniform temperature distribution and efficient use of space, making them ideal for high-volume production. In contrast, horizontal furnaces offer easier access to substrates and can accommodate larger wafers, providing flexibility in processing. Both types are crucial in semiconductor manufacturing for doping and thermal oxidation, catering to different production needs and material handling requirements, thereby influencing market dynamics and technology advancements.</p></p>
