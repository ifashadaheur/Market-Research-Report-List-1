<p><p>The yeast storage tank market comprises two primary types: yeast pressure storage tanks and yeast non-pressurized storage tanks. Yeast pressure storage tanks are designed to maintain a pressurized environment, ensuring optimal yeast viability and reducing the risk of contamination. In contrast, yeast non-pressurized storage tanks operate at ambient pressure, making them suitable for applications where pressure is not a concern. Both types are essential for the brewing and fermentation industries, promoting efficient yeast management and production processes.</p></p>
